How important is website design?

The website is a point of contact for your brand, a business tool and a place to generate important conversions. A great website can be a growth engine for your business, while a weak site can hurt your brand.

But premium web design is difficult. Today's visitors have high expectations. You just have a few seconds – maybe milliseconds – to convince visitors that your site is worth their time.

With years of experience creating websites, Codigency builds sites that exceed audience expectations. We create high-performance websites with a focus on strategic planning, compelling visual design and flawless user experience to deliver superior results.

How to build a better converting website:

Each website is designed to achieve different goals – from sales and awareness to engagement. To create a website that actually achieves these goals, it's important to design it the right way.

Design for your users

Put the needs of site users first. If you don't know what users need, ask them. As a user-centric agency, this principle is the foundation of everything we create.

Design for a purpose

A great site contains beauty and functionality. It should look good but must also be built with a clear vision of its most important goals and conversion points.

Design for discoverability

The site should be designed so people can find it – starting with search engines. We build technically optimized sites with attractive, relevant landing pages.

Our Website Design Process

Codigency's website design methodology has been tested and improved over years of successful projects

Discovery + User Research

We start by understanding your business, talking to stakeholders and analyzing existing data. Our goal is to understand your users through customer interviews, surveys, and testing.

Strategy

We align site structure, content and functionality with business objectives. Our strategists collaborate with UX, design and content experts to make data-driven recommendations.

Planning

We convert strategy into actionable plans with sitemaps and wireframes that show structure and page hierarchy. All concepts are tested with users for continuous improvement.

Design

We bring your brand to life with visually stunning designs. Whether you have strict brand guidelines or need brand development, we create responsive designs optimized for all devices.

Copywriting

Our copywriters craft compelling content that captures attention, tells your story, and converts visitors. We write for both humans and search engines.

SEO

SEO is integrated from the start. We ensure your site is discoverable, relevant, and follows best practices through careful review of sitemaps, content, and technical elements.

Development & Launch

Our developers build your site with clean code and rigorous testing. We provide thorough quality assurance to ensure your site looks and works perfectly before launch.
